Sarabhai Institute of Science and Technology

Welcome to the official website of Sarabhai Institute of Science and Technology (SIST). Named after the pioneer of Space Science & technology in India, this institute was set up to fulfill a dream of the great visionary, Dr.Vikram. A.Sarabhai to mould high quality experts in frontier engineering disciplines. The High-tech engineering college named “SARABHAI INSTITUTE OF SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY” is a self- financing institution established by the Space Engineers Welfare Society (SEWELS) with the approval from the Government of Kerala. The campus, “SARABHAI INSTITUTE OF SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY” is at VELLANAD, a rustic village in the outskirts of Thiruvananthapuram city. A vast stretch of land comprising about 25 acres forms the campus having all the prime infrastructural facilities including hostels, canteen, library, fully-equipped labs, etc. With its scenic beauty and virgin surroundings, this typical location gives the right ambiance for this prestigious institution
